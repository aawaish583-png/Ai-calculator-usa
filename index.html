Adsense RPM Calculator

What is RPM?

RPM means Revenue Per 1000 impressions. It shows how much money you earn per 1000 pageviews from Google Adsense or any ad network.

Why RPM is Important?

RPM helps bloggers and website owners understand their income performance. A higher RPM means better monetization.

How This Calculator Works

This calculator uses CTR and CPC to estimate earnings. It is useful for Adsense publishers, bloggers, and digital marketers.
